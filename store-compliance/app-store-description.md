# App Store Description - Doomscrolls

## Short Description (80 chars)
Transform mindless scrolling into meaningful reading with classical literature.

## Full Description

**If you're going to scroll, scroll through wisdom.**

We spend hours scrolling through social media - what if that time fed your mind instead of draining it? Doomscrolls transforms the addictive infinite scroll experience into something meaningful: an endless stream of humanity's greatest writings.

Doomscrolls helps you discover timeless wisdom from history's greatest minds, one passage at a time.

**FEATURES:**
- Infinite feed of curated passages from classical literature, philosophy, poetry, and wisdom texts
- 10+ million passages from 7,600+ authors and 17,000+ works
- Smart personalization that learns your preferences
- Follow your favorite authors - from Marcus Aurelius to Emily Dickinson
- Create reading lists to save and organize passages
- Full work reader - read complete books passage-by-passage
- Search across the entire classical canon
- Beautiful dark and light themes

**CURATED CATEGORIES:**
- Philosophy & Stoicism
- Poetry & Verse
- Classic Fiction
- Religious & Spiritual Texts
- Essays & Speeches
- Drama & Theatre
- Ancient to Modern literature

**PRIVACY FIRST:**
- No account required to browse
- Optional account for syncing across devices
- Your data is never sold
- See our Privacy Policy for details

**FREE TO USE:**
Doomscrolls is completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls.
